\item Pooled annualised alpha (\%) for actively managed funds under three factor specifications. \textit{Carhart 4-Factor}: MKT-RF, SMB, HML, MOM (\citealt{Carhart1997}; main text baseline). \textit{FF 6-Factor}: MKT-RF, SMB, HML, RMW, CMA, MOM (\citealt{FamaFrench2015} five-factor model plus Carhart momentum). \textit{Carhart + PSL}: Carhart four-factor augmented with the \citet{PastorStambaugh2003} traded liquidity factor. EW: equal-weighted cross-sectional mean across per-fund alphas. VW: value-weighted cross-sectional mean using time-series mean TNA as fund weights. Net returns are gross returns less one-twelfth of the static annual expense ratio each month following \citet{BarrasScailletWermers2010}. $N$: number of active funds with at least 24 monthly observations; $T_{\max}$: maximum number of monthly observations per fund. SMB is held constant across specifications at the \citet{FamaFrench1993} construction (single $2\times3$ size--BM sort) rather than switching to the \citet{FamaFrench2015} construction (average of three $2\times3$ sorts on size-BM, size-OP, and size-Inv); the two SMB series correlate above 0.97 over the sample window, and holding SMB constant isolates the marginal contribution of RMW and CMA. Sample: Dec 1994--Feb 2026 for Carhart and FF 6-Factor; Dec 1994--Dec 2024 for Carhart + PSL (constrained by the December 2024 cutoff of the Pastor liquidity data release as of the analysis date).
